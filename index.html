🔄 Indirect Speech Exercise

Change the direct speech into indirect (reported) speech. Suitable for S2 / Grade 8.

📖 Quick Review

When changing direct speech to indirect speech, remember:

TenseDirect → Indirect
Present Simple"I like it." → He said he liked it.
Present Continuous"I am doing" → He said he was doing
Past Simple"I saw it." → He said he had seen it.
Will"I will go." → He said he would go.
Can"I can swim." → He said he could swim.
Must"You must try." → He said I had to try.
Now → Then"I am busy now." → He said he was busy then.
Today → That day"I called today." → He said he had called that day.
